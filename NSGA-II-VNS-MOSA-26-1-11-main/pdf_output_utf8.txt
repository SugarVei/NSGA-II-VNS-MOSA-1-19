为回应审稿人关于"MILP模型有而不验、模型正确性无法确认"的核心质疑，本节设计了一套基于ε-约
束法（ε-constraint 
method）的三目标精确求解验证框架，通过商业求解器 
Gurobi
对若干小规模算例进行精确求解，以获得参考帕累托前沿（Reference 
Pareto
Front），并将元启发式算法 
NSGA-II-VNS-MOSA
的求解质量与之进行定量对比，从而同时实现两个目的：（1）验证 MILP 模型约束体系的逻辑正确
性；（2）论证精确算法在中大规模实例下的计算不可行性，为本文采用元启发式方法提供充分的必
要性论据。

三、三目标精确求解方法（ε-Constraint Method）
由于 
Gurobi 
内置多目标功能为层次化优化，不支持帕累托前沿直接求解，本文采用经典
ε-约束法（Mavrotas, 2009）获取真实帕累托解集。以最大完工时间 Z1 为主目标最小化，将总能耗
Z2 和总人力成本 Z3 的上界分别设定为 ε2 和 ε3，构造如下约束优化子问题：
min ZI(x)
s.t. ZI(x) ≤ εI, ZI(x) ≤ εI, x ∈ F
对目标 Z2 和 Z3 各划分10个均匀分布的 ε 水平，构成10×10=100个子问题。每个子问题时限600秒
（V4、V5可延长至1800秒），记录可行解及其 
MIP
Gap。所有子问题求解完毕后，对所得解集进行非支配排序，取非支配层构成精确参考帕累托前沿
PFexact。
参考文献：Mavrotas, G. (2009). Effective implementation of the ε-constraint method in multi-objective
mathematical programming problems. Applied Mathematics and Computation, 213(2), 455–465.

5.3. MILP Model Correctness Verification
5.3.1. Motivation and Design
While the MILP model established in Section 3 provides the formal mathematical foundation of this
study, its correctness cannot be assumed without empirical validation. Accordingly, this section
presents a systematic verification framework using Gurobi 10.0, applied to five small-scale
instances (V1–V5) derived by proportional truncation from the real-world factory dataset described
in Section 6.1. The verification serves dual purposes: (i) confirming the logical consistency of the
constraint system (Equations 7–24), and (ii) quantifying the optimality gap between the
metaheuristic solutions and the exact Pareto front, thereby justifying the necessity of the proposed
heuristic approach for medium- and large-scale instances.
5.3.2. Exact Solution Methodology
Since the problem involves three objectives, the exact Pareto front cannot be obtained directly by
Gurobi's built-in multi-objective solver, which operates hierarchically. We therefore adopt the
augmented epsilon-constraint method (Mavrotas, 2009). Specifically, the makespan Z_1 is
minimized as the primary objective, while the total energy consumption Z_2 and total labor cost Z_3
are bounded by parameters epsilon_2 and epsilon_3, respectively. Ten uniformly distributed
epsilon-levels are applied to each of Z_2 and Z_3, yielding a grid of 10 x 10 = 100 subproblems per
instance. Each subproblem is solved with a time limit of 600 seconds (extended to 1,800 seconds
for V4 and V5). Feasible solutions obtained across all subproblems are aggregated and filtered by
non-dominated sorting to construct the exact reference Pareto front PF_exact. The verification
instances share the same stage structure as the main experiments: three stages, two parallel
machines per stage, three processing speeds, and three worker skill levels.

五、实施要点（Implementation Notes）
5.1 Gurobi 建模核心代码结构
本文 MILP 为三目标模型，需在 Python 接口中循环调用 ε-constraint，核心伪代码如下：
for p in range(num_eps_2):
    for q in range(num_eps_3):
        model = build_MILP_model(instance_data)
        model.setObjective(Z1, GRB.MINIMIZE)
        model.addConstr(Z2 <= eps_2[p])
        model.addConstr(Z3 <= eps_3[q])
        model.Params.TimeLimit = 600
        model.optimize()
        if model.status in [GRB.OPTIMAL, GRB.TIME_LIMIT]:
            if model.SolCount > 0:
                record_solution(model)
